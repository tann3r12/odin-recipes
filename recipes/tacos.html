The Best Taco Recipe

Welcome! You have just stumbled upon the single most important recipe known to humankind. In this short article I will be walking you through some basic steps to make the absolute BEST tacos. Prepare to have your mind blown.

Ingredients

  • 1lbs ground beef
  • 1 can of refried beans
  • 1 tomato
  • 1 head of iceberg lettuce
  • sour cream
  • salsa
  • 10 corn tortilla shells

Steps

  1. Brown ground beef in large pan with stove set to medium-high.
  2. Warm refried beans in medium pot with stove set to medium. Stir often
  3. Once ground beef is brown and no pink is visible, drain grease from pan thoroughly.
  4. Once beans are hot, turn off stove.
  5. Cut tomatoes in to small squares
  6. Shred lettuce
  7. Add ingredients to shells as desired, ensuring you add runny ingredients (beans and sour cream) first to ensure the perfect spread.
  8. Serve and enjoy!
