Heart Attack Spaghetti Recipe

Welcome to the end of your life! In this simple recipe we will be walking you through the steps to create spaghetti so good and so unhealthy it will literally end your life! Wowza!

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti Noodles
  • Ragu Spaghetti Meat Sauce
  • 1lbs Ground Beef
  • 1/2 Cup Butter
  • 1tbsp Garlic Salt

Steps

  1. Brown ground beef in pan on medium-high heat
  2. Bring large pot of water to boil on high heat, then put spaghetti noodles in pot and reduce heat to medium, stirring often.
  3. Once ground beef is browned, drain grease. Once drained stir in Ragu meat flavored sauce and warm on low heat, stirring often.
  4. Check noodles. Once soft, drain water from pot. Once drained, stir in 1/2 cup of Butter and 1tbsp garlic salt.
  5. Once sauce and meat is warm, turn off heat
  6. Put noodles on plate and smother with sauce
  7. Have 911 pre-dialed on your phone for the moment when your arteries clogged
