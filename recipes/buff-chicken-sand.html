Buffalo Chicken Sandwich Recipe

Ingredients

  • Thick White Bread
  • 3 Chicken Breasts
  • Cookies Buffalo Sauce
  • Block of Cream Cheese
  • Sliced Provolone Cheese
  • Ranch (Side)

Steps

  1. Place chicken breasts in pressure cooker and set timer to 20 minutes. Press start.
  2. Once chicken is finished cooking, set out to cool for 10 minutes.
  3. Once cooled, shred chicken by hand or with cutlery.
  4. Place shredded chicken in to bowl, add full block of cream cheese, and 1 cup of Cookies Buffalo Sauce. Mix thoroughly.
  5. Place 1 slice of provolone cheese on bread,then add buffalo chicken mixture.
  6. Place second piece of bread on top, butter both sides of sandwich, then place full sandwich in panini press for 3 minutes.
  7. Once golden brown on both sides, serve with side of ranch.
  8. Enjoy!
